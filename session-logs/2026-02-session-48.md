## Decisions Made

1. **Translation approach:** Parallel sub-agents (6 batches of 8 locales) is the fastest approach. Single agents with all 44 locales hit the 32K output token limit. Documented in CLAUDE.md as the standard protocol.
2. **SHARE_ACCESS_PASSWORD:** Set to "reallyglobal" â€” easy to remember, shared with Simon
3. **SHARE_IDENTITY_KEY:** Generated 32-byte random hex key for AES-256-GCM encryption
4. **Env vars not in env.ts:** Deliberately kept out of startup validation so missing vars don't crash the whole app â€” they only affect share routes
