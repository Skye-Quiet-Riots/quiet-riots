### Discoveries

- **Squash-merge leaves phantom commits:** After PR #43 was squash-merged, the local branch still showed its commit as "ahead of main" even though the content was on main. This is expected — squash creates a new commit hash. The `git log origin/main..HEAD` check sees these phantom commits. Not a problem as long as you create a fresh branch rather than trying to push more to the old one.
