- **Bot action names preserved:** `contribute` and `get_campaigns` bot actions kept for SKILL.md/TOOLS.md/OpenClaw backward compatibility. Renaming requires SKILL.md update + session clearing — separate PR.

- **i18n batches of 8 locales hit prompt-too-long:** Retried with batches of 4 — worked perfectly.
